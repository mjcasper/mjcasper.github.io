Locating a Test Site

Resources

Everyone has the option to sit for their exams either at UB or an instructor approved off-campus testing center.

To help locate an off-campus test site we have a few recommended resources:

  • Our list of past test sites in New York State
  • National College Testing Association (NCTA)
  • SUNY Exam Proctoring System

These are only recommendations meant to give you a starting point in your search; you are free to use other testing centers not listed in these resources as long as they satisfy the conditions described in the Exam Location Policy.

Comments About These Resources

  • These websites should only be used to help locate a potential testing center and its contact information.
  • You should not use them to schedule your appointments. Instead, contact the testing center directly to schedule your appointments.
  • On the NCTA website you can disregard where it says “Testing Center Certified”. This distinction is irrelevant for our purposes.
